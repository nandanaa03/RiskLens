About RiskLens

RiskLens is an intelligent Public Health Surveillance platform designed to analyze India's National Family Health Survey (NFHS-5) dataset. The platform integrates ETL, data cleaning, composite health risk analysis, BFS-based state clustering, statistical visualizations, and GIS mapping into a unified dashboard that assists policymakers in identifying vulnerable regions and prioritizing healthcare interventions.
